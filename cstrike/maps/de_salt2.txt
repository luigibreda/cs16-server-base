Salt 2 - Bomb/Defuse
Map and textures by SuperJer
www.superjer.com

This map was designed for 5v5 matches,
but will support up to 32 players.

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent Terrorists
from bombing either of the two bomb
targets. Team members must defuse the
bomb if it is planted.

Terrorists: The Terrorist carrying the
C4 must successfully detonate the bomb
at one of the two targets, or
eliminate the enemy team.

(Press FIRE to continue)
